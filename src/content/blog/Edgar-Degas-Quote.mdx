Just try to keep in mind something [Edgar Degas](https://www.washingtonpost.com/entertainment/museums/degas-had-a-gift-for-conveying-the-truth--even-when-he-was-making-it-all-up/2019/10/30/8e3ed8e2-fb2c-11e9-8190-6be4deb56e01_story.html?itid=lk_inline_enhanced-template) once said (a mantra to live by, in my judgment): “What a delightful thing is the conversation of specialists! One understands absolutely nothing and it’s charming.”
